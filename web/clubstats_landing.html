FAQ

Things clubs ask before they order.

Do you need our Play Cricket login?

Yes — briefly. The full stats page on Play Cricket sits behind sign-in, so we need access to capture your numbers. We'll arrange this securely on a quick screen-share (10 minutes), or you can paste a one-time login into a password manager link we send you. We don't store credentials.

How current are the stats?

On setup we pull every match from the last four seasons. During the season, we re-capture every Sunday night so Monday morning your URL is fresh.

Can I include the junior section too?

Yes — Under-9 through Under-18 cricket can be added as a bolt-on for £49. We add a separate "Junior" mode to the dashboard so seniors and juniors stay clearly separated.

Who can see the URL?

Anyone you share it with — no login. Treat it like an unlisted YouTube video: not advertised, not indexed by Google, but viewable by anyone with the URL. If you'd rather have password protection, we offer that as a £30 add-on.

What's included in the £99/year renewal?

Weekly stats refresh through every season, all new feature releases (we ship most weeks), continued hosting, and email support. If a season ends and you don't want to renew, the dashboard stops updating but stays live for the rest of the calendar year as a free archive.

What about T20s, midweek leagues, friendlies?

All competitions Play Cricket records show up — we let you filter by League / Cup / Friendly / League & Cup combined. Indoor and Pairs use slightly different scoring; if your club plays those, mention it on the order form and we'll configure separately.

Can we put it on our own club website?

Absolutely. The URL embeds in any club website via a single iframe tag. We'll send you the snippet on delivery.

Refunds?

If the dashboard doesn't go live in 48 hours, full refund. If you change your mind in the first 30 days, full refund. After that, annual renewal can be cancelled any time and you keep access through the end of the paid year.
